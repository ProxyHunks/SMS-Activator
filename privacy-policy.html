Overview & Scope

The SMS Activator extension is published by ProxyHunks (Hunks SMS) and is available on the Chrome Web Store. Its purpose is to give users quick access to the Hunks SMS virtual phone number and proxy service directly from their browser — allowing them to browse available numbers, initiate purchases, top up their wallet, activate proxies, and receive SMS verification codes without leaving the current tab.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices for the extension only. Our main website privacy policy is available at vnumbers.proxyhunks.xyz.

How We Use Your Data

Data collected by the extension is used exclusively for the following purposes:

Service delivery — authenticating your account, displaying your wallet balance, processing number and proxy purchases, and relaying incoming SMS codes to your extension popup.

Fraud prevention — detecting and blocking abuse of the service, including repeated failed purchases and bot-like behaviour.

Technical support — diagnosing errors and responding to support requests you initiate via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email.

Service improvement — aggregated, anonymous usage patterns (e.g., which countries or services are most requested) to improve the product. This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the virtual number and proxy service.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

Upstream SMS & proxy providers — to fulfil a number or proxy purchase, the selected service name and country code are passed to our supplier APIs. No personally identifiable information about you (name, email, phone) is shared with them.

M-Pesa / Safaricom — payment processing for wallet top-ups is handled entirely by Safaricom's STK Push infrastructure. We do not receive, store, or transmit your M-Pesa PIN. Note: M-Pesa payments are initiated through the Hunks SMS website or WhatsApp bot, not directly through the Chrome extension.

Cryptomus — if you choose to top up via crypto, invoice creation and payment confirmation are handled by Cryptomus. We do not receive your wallet's private keys or seed phrase.

Legal obligations — we may disclose data if required to do so by applicable law or a valid legal order. We will notify you to the extent permitted by law.

No data is shared with advertising networks, data brokers, analytics platforms, or social media companies.

Data Storage & Security

Local storage — session tokens and preferences are stored using the Chrome Extension Storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data stays on your device and is not accessible to other extensions or websites.

Server-side storage — account and transaction data is stored on our backend servers. We implement the following security measures:

Encryption in transit: All communication between the extension and our API uses HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.

Encryption at rest: Sensitive account data is encrypted in our database.

Access controls: Server access is restricted to authorised personnel only, using role-based access controls and audit logging.

Log retention: Server-side request logs are automatically purged after 30 days.

SMS message content (your verification codes) is relayed to you in real time and is not stored on our servers beyond the 20-minute active window. After that window closes, the message content is permanently and automatically deleted.
